## A Survey of Large Language Models: Exploring the Advancements and Impacts of Scaled-Up Language Models
Summary: This research paper aims to survey the field of large language models (LLMs), shedding light on their significant advancements in recent times. The study explores four major aspects of LLMs: pre-training, adaptation tuning, utilization, and capacity evaluation. Additionally, it delves into the challenges and future prospects of LLMs, providing insights that could further drive progress in the development and application of these powerful language models.

Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.18223
